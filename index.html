PANHANDLE PEDAL FISHING

Guided kayak fishing trips on high end foot pedaled kayaks out of Pensacola and Navarre. Inshore, offshore, and eco tour trips available! Book an adventure with us today!

Inshore Fishing

Inshore trips targeting redfish, speckled trout, flounder, sheepshead, and much more! Our guides will work out a trip custom just for you depending on your preference of live bait or artificial fishing and what species you want to target. Also ask about our night time trips to beat the Florida heat!

OFFSHORE FISHING

The gulf of Mexico offers some of the best fishing in the world and our section up here in the panhandle is no exception! We spend our time offshore chasing King Mackerel, Cobia, Mahi-Mahi, Triggerfish, and Red Snapper, Grouper, Sailfish and more! The trips start before the sunrises and end just before the heat becomes to much to handle. If you’re willing to start your day early, summer is perfect time of the year to schedule an offshore adventure!
